📖 About This Project

The Governance – Data Transparency AI Dashboard improves transparency in government public spending by presenting budget and expenditure data in a clear, interactive format. It shows department-wise allocation, actual spending, remaining balances, and overall financial performance for FY 2025-26 at a glance.

The goal is to make public finance easy to follow for citizens, students, and officials alike — turning raw budget figures into insights that support accountability. Looking ahead, AI-driven analysis can help flag unusual spending patterns early, forecast future budget needs, and support more informed decisions in public governance.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Improve transparency around how public funds are allocated and spent.
  • Present department-wise budgets in a clear, visual format.
  • Help citizens and officials quickly gauge spending efficiency.
  • Flag high-spending or underutilized departments early.
  • Deliver a simple, accessible dashboard usable without prior training.

⭐ Key Features

  • ✔ Real-time summary of total budget, spend, and remaining balance
  • ✔ Department-wise breakdown across 6 sectors
  • ✔ Interactive bar chart comparing budget vs. actual spend
  • ✔ Pie chart showing each department's share of the total budget
  • ✔ Searchable, filterable spending table
  • ✔ Downloadable plain-text spending report
  • ✔ AI-style narrative analysis of spending trends

🚀 Future Scope

  • Connect to real government open-data APIs for live figures.
  • AI-based budget forecasting for upcoming fiscal years.
  • Year-over-year comparison charts across multiple fiscal years.
  • Exportable PDF reports with embedded charts.
  • Fully responsive mobile experience.
  • District-level and scheme-level drill-down views.
